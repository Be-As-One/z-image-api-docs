## How Credits Work

We use a credit-based system. Each generation consumes credits based on model and parameters.

**Credit Pricing:**
- 1 credit = $0.01 USD
- Minimum purchase: 100 credits ($1)
- Credits never expire

## Video Generation Costs

| Duration | Resolution | Credits | Cost |
|----------|-----------|---------|------|

## Failed Tasks

**We don't charge for failures.** If a task fails due to our system issues, credits are automatically refunded.

**User-caused failures** (invalid prompts, unsupported formats) consume credits. Check parameters before submitting.
